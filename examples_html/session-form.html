Session Form

Multi-step form with typed state transitions (SessionExec pattern)

Expected: A multi-step registration form. Step 1: enter Name, Step 2: enter Email, Step 3: enter Age. Step 4: review (shows all entered data). Step 5: "Registration Complete". Each step has a progress indicator (e.g. "Step 1/3"). Next advances to the next step, Back goes back. Each phase only saves its own field. The form follows a typed session protocol.
