Tetris

Chord-based controls: all 10 fingers, zero wasted moves

Position: A S D F G H J K L ; → columns 0–9 (home row, direct addressing).
Rotation: toggle: C ±90°, M ±180° (each press flips one bit, all 4 states reachable).
Alt: ←→ move, ↑ rotate. Esc pause. 0–9 start level. Enter start.
