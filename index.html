Architecture (Svelte-style)

  • bindF — reactive text bindings
  • when/foreach — conditional & lists
  • Event — subscriptions
  • Cmd — commands (HTTP, etc.)
  • No Virtual DOM — O(bindings) updates
  • Type-safe templates in Agda
